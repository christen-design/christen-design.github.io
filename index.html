Brand Identity & Campaign

Shop Local Campaign

“Shop Local” re-established Reading Terminal Market as the grocery for Philly locals, not just tourists. Christen served as Art Director & Graphic Designer—leading concept, copy, photography, and execution. Multi-channel rollout: bus ads, direct mailers, Metro wraps, recipe cards.
Mission: “Provide fresh foods to the people of Philadelphia and curate an environment that celebrates the diversity of its citizens.”

Project Details

  • Role: Art Director, Graphic Designer
  • Led concept, copy, design, and photography
  • Assets: bus shelter ads, direct mailers, seasonal recipes, banners
  • Goal: “Fresh food for Philly—just one ride away.”

How I Work

I craft brands and campaigns with empathy, intention, and joy. Every project starts with listening: to a client’s story, the community’s needs, and the heart of the brand. I believe design should be bold, inclusive, and always people-centered.

Discovery

Learn the client’s vision, research context, define real goals.

Concept

Explore directions, sketch, build out storyboards or wireframes.

Execution

Iterate and refine: visuals, copy, assets, UX, and handoff with precision.
